Discover All You Can Do on GoSub: Airtime, Data, Bills, and More!

Hey there!

Are you a new or existing subscriber wondering what exactly you can do on GoSub? Well, this blog post is for you. Welcome to the tribe once again!

On GoSub, you can do almost everything that involves buying or selling airtime and data, including:

1. Buy & Sell Airtime and Data

Whether you are an MTN, Airtel, GLO, or 9Mobile subscriber, GoSub makes it easy for you to buy and sell airtime at the best rates. Our platform ensures you get the value you deserve without breaking the bank.

2. Convert Airtime to Cash

Have excess airtime you don't need? Convert it to cash quickly and conveniently with GoSub. Our conversion rates are unbeatable, ensuring you get the most out of your airtime.

3. Become a GoSub Reseller

Looking to make some extra income? Become a GoSub agent by reselling airtime or data. Data plan reselling means buying data plans at a cheaper rate, and then selling to others at a higher rate than what you used to buy. It's a fantastic opportunity to earn while providing a valuable service to your community.

4. Pay Electricity Bills

With GoSub, you can easily settle your electricity bills right from the comfort of your home. We support payments for major electricity providers across Nigeria, ensuring that your power stays on without stress.

5. Cable TV Subscription

Keep your favourite shows going with seamless cable TV subscription payments. Whether it’s DSTV, GOTV, or Startimes, you can recharge your account in just a few clicks using GoSub.


In addition to these core services, GoSub is constantly innovating to provide more value to our users. We are committed to making airtime, data, electricity, and cable TV transactions as smooth and affordable as possible.


We offer the best rates you can find in the market. Plus, our payout process is incredibly fast, ensuring you get your money instantly. Trade your airtime with us this weekend for unbelievable rates and instant payment.


Our platform is designed to be seamless and user-friendly, making it easy to manage your airtime, data, and other utility needs on the go.


Our Customer Support team is active 24/7 to assist you. Whether you need help with a transaction or have inquiries about our services, we are here to help.


We look forward to serving you and helping you make the most out of your airtime, data, electricity, and cable TV subscriptions. If you haven’t already, install our app and start exploring all the fantastic features we offer.
